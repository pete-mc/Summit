Bulk Calender Form

Welcome to our Bulk Calendar Form. This simple interface allows you to efficiently manage and schedule multiple events with ease.
Use the 'Add Item' button to insert new events. To edit event details, simply click on the desired field in the table. This will activate the inline editor, enabling you to input or modify information directly within the row. After making changes, click elsewhere to save your updates automatically.

Once you have set the first entry the start and end date will pick the same date and time 7 days later helping you to complete you full term plan.

When you are ready to save all the records into Terrain press Sumbit.
